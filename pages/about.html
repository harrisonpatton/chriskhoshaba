ABOUT ME

Chris Khoshaba
Actor/Singer

I grew up in an Assyrian and Italian household in Skokie, IL. I found acting in high school, where I also found my forever partner. I’m a 6 '4 actor/singer, and graduate of the University of Illinois in Urbana-Champaign’s Acting program. Since graduating, I’ve worked at multiple theatres in Chicago, including The Goodman, Porchlight Music Theatre, The House Theatre of Chicago, Silk Road Rising, and more. I love new work that genuinely highlights the SWANASA community, and any opportunity to sing.

When not acting, I'm training at Orangetheory Fitness, and a cat dad to two tuxedo cats named “Chicken and Waffles”.
